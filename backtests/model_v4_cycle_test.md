## Caveats
- **Survivorship:** all lists are survivors only (no delisted names) — early-cycle returns are upward-biased.
- **Curated list is recency-skewed:** many model_v4 names IPO'd late, so its early cycles trade a thin subset.
- **Mostly in-sample:** model_v4 was tuned ~2018–2026; recent cycles are the least independent.
- **Hedge inactive before 2011** (SOXS did not exist) — pre-2011 broad+hedge equals broad.
- **Hedge** modeled on QQQ/SPY/SOXS with flat 10bp costs; shines in crash/vol-spike cycles, mild drag in calm ones.
